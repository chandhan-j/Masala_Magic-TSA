Paneer Biryani

What is Paneer Biryani?

Paneer Biryani is a flavorful, aromatic dish that combines marinated paneer cubes with long-grain basmati rice and a rich blend of Indian spices. This one-pot meal is a delightful fusion of textures and flavors, making it a favorite among biryani lovers.

Ingredients:

  • Paneer (Indian cottage cheese)
  • Basmati rice
  • Onions, tomatoes, and yogurt
  • Whole spices (cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, bay leaves)
  • Fresh herbs like mint and coriander

How It's Made:

Paneer cubes are marinated in a mix of yogurt, spices, and herbs. The basmati rice is cooked separately with whole spices for added aroma. These are then layered and cooked together using the dum method, infusing the rice and paneer with incredible flavor.

Perfect Pairing:

Paneer Biryani is best served with raita, salad, or a side of pickles for a complete meal.
