Gobi Manchuria

What is Gobi Manchuria?

Gobi Manchuria is a popular Indo-Chinese dish made from crispy fried cauliflower florets tossed in a tangy, spicy, and flavorful sauce. This vegetarian delight is perfect as an appetizer or a snack, often enjoyed at parties or gatherings.

Ingredients:

  • Cauliflower (Gobi)
  • All-purpose flour and cornstarch (for the batter)
  • Garlic, ginger, and green chilies
  • Soy sauce, tomato ketchup, and chili sauce
  • Spring onions (for garnish)

How It's Made:

The cauliflower is coated in a spiced batter and deep-fried until crispy. The sauce is prepared separately using garlic, ginger, soy sauce, and chili sauce, then combined with the fried cauliflower. Garnished with spring onions, this dish is both flavorful and aromatic.

Perfect Pairing:

Serve Gobi Manchuria with fried rice, noodles, or enjoy it as a standalone snack.
