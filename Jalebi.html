Jalebi

What is Jalebi?

Jalebi is a popular Indian sweet made by deep-frying batter into spiral shapes and soaking them in sugar syrup. Known for its crispy texture and syrupy sweetness, Jalebi is a beloved treat served during festivals and celebrations.

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Yogurt (for fermentation)
  • Sugar and water (for syrup)
  • Saffron or cardamom (for flavor)
  • Ghee or oil (for frying)

How It's Made:

A fermented batter made from flour and yogurt is piped into hot oil in circular patterns. The fried jalebis are then soaked in a warm sugar syrup flavored with cardamom or saffron, giving them their signature taste and glossy appearance.

Perfect Pairing:

Jalebi is often enjoyed with a glass of warm milk, rabri, or as is for a delightful dessert experience.
