Energy Efficient Equipment

What is Energy-Efficient Equipment?

Energy-efficient sustainable operations at Masala Magic revolve around the acquisition of energy-saving appliances and equipment. We use induction cooktops and energy-saving ovens for cooking. We use smart refrigerators—energy-saving appliances—for refrigeration and freezing. These energy-saving alternatives require less energy draw; however, they still provide the same temperature settings and ranges as required for any restaurant.

Why It Matters:

Utilizing this equipment reduces greenhouse gas emissions but also reduces energy costs in the long run. We also have a water-saving dishwasher and low-wattage light bulbs. Since most energy-efficient appliances were gifted from Masala Magic in the registry, it goes to show that energy efficiency goes hand in hand with efficient eating.

Our Commitment to Energy Efficiency:

These appliances help our sustainable efforts but simultaneously keep us running cost-effectively without compromising our ability to continually serve fresh, high-quality meals to our patrons.
