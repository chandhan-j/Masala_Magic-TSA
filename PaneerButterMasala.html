Paneer Butter Masala

What is Paneer Butter Masala?

Paneer Butter Masala is a classic North Indian curry made with soft cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) simmered in a rich, creamy, and flavorful tomato-based gravy. It’s a favorite dish in Indian restaurants and is loved for its smooth texture and delightful flavors.

Ingredients:

  • Paneer (Indian cottage cheese)
  • Tomatoes and cashews (for the gravy)
  • Butter and cream
  • Ginger, garlic, and spices (coriander, cumin, garam masala)
  • Kasturi methi (dried fenugreek leaves) for garnish

How It's Made:

The rich gravy is made by blending sautéed tomatoes, cashews, and spices into a smooth paste. The paneer cubes are gently simmered in this creamy sauce, enriched with butter and cream, and finished with a garnish of kasturi methi for a fragrant, flavorful touch.

Perfect Pairing:

Paneer Butter Masala pairs perfectly with naan, roti, or steamed basmati rice.
